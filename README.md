## Sponsorships

I accept sponsorships sparingly and only with companies or small businesses that I have personally interacted with, either through direct communication or personal use/interest in their products.  

### Sponsor Vetting
I am selective about sponsors and will never promote anything that I consider scammy or not valuable to viewers.

### Paid Videos / Product Showcase Videos
I never produce paid videos where a sponsor pays me to provide commentary. My videos are faceless, and all information is presented visually or via text overlays. Sponsored products are always clearly disclosed and chosen based on genuine interest and relevance.
